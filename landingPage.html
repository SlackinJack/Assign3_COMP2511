Who am I?

Born 'Steve Smith' on April 9th, 1975, in L.A. California, I was deeply involved as a child in musical productions. Ranging from piano to guitar, and even the ukulele, I mastered 7 instruments by the age of 9. I performed very well in band in middle school, the Los Angeles Academy Middle School, and was often sent on trips to perform and represent them. We, as a school, won multiple musical performance awards, including The USC Thornton School of Music Award, in which secured our band team a scholarship when we attended university.

You may have noticed that my name was originally Steve. The move to change my name to John came about in my late-30's, in which a close childhood friend of mine, John Applewood, had passed away from a vehicular accident. He was the friend who was 'always there when you needed him'. A helpful and creative person he was. He was also a band member from the same middle school. In honor and tribute to his passing, I made the switch from Steve to John.
